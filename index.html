About Section

Riyad Restaurant was established 9 years ago, on March 12, 2017, in the heart of Dhaka, Dhanmondi.

It was founded by three food-loving university friends—Abir, Raiyan, and Samia—who pooled their savings and brought unique recipes to life right after graduation.

Restaurant Overview at a Glance

  • Founded On: March 12, 2017
  • Founders: Abir Ahmed, Raiyan Karim, and Samia Rahman
  • First Branch: Dhanmondi, Dhaka
  • Core Concept: Blending authentic Bangladeshi spices with Western fusion cuisine

How It All Started

The beginning was far from easy. The restaurant started in a cozy, small outlet with just 5 tables and a single chef. However, their signature dishes—the "Honey-Glazed Crispy Chicken" and the innovative "Kacchi Pizza"—became an instant hit among local foodies.

"We didn't just want to sell food; we wanted people to experience a royal burst of flavors with every single bite."— Abir Ahmed (Co-founder)

Where It Stands Today

What started as a tiny eatery in 2017 has grown into one of Bangladesh's most popular food chains by 2026. Today, "Riyad Restaurant" boasts 5 successful branches across Dhaka and Chattogram, serving thousands of happy food lovers every day.

Here is the information about the food varieties and the reasons why people visit Riyad Restaurant written in English:

What Kind of Food is Available

Riyad Restaurant is best known for its unique fusion and signature dishes. The menu mainly features three types of categories:

1. Fusion Food: The restaurant's most popular items are the "Kacchi Pizza" and "Honey-Glazed Crispy Chicken." They also serve delicious pastas and burgers infused with local Bangladeshi spices.

2. Continental and Fast Food: A wide variety of premium pizzas, crispy fried chicken, peri-peri chicken, and cheese-loaded fries.

3. Signature Desserts and Drinks: To satisfy your sweet tooth, they offer freshly baked waffles, chocolate lava cakes, and a selection of refreshing mocktails and shakes.

Why People Come Here

People do not visit Riyad Restaurant just for the food, but for the overall experience. Here are the main reasons why it is always crowded:

1. Unique and Innovative Flavors: The blend of traditional Bangladeshi spices with Western cuisine creates a taste that cannot be found anywhere else. This unique twist makes it a massive hit, especially among the youth.

2. Premium Quality at a Pocket-Friendly Price: The quality and portion sizes of the food are excellent compared to the price. It is the perfect spot for students and families looking for a great meal without breaking the bank.

3. Great Ambiance and Hospitality: The interior of the restaurant is cozy, modern, and highly Instagram-friendly. Combined with the warm behavior of the staff and quick service, it makes guests feel right at home.

4. 9 Years of Trust: From its beginning in 2017 up to 2026, the restaurant has never compromised on food quality. This consistency has built a loyal community of food lovers who keep coming back.
